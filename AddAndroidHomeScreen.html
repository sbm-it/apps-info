Simply follow the instructions below to add a website bookmark to your home screen. This should work on all Android devices, but the screenshots below have been taken on a Samsung Galaxy S6:




    1.    Launch Chrome for Android and open the website https://sbm-it.github.io/apps:

    2.    Tap the menu button and tap “Add to homescreen”:

    3.    Change to bookmark name so that it suits you.Once you’re done, click the “Add” button:

    5.    The icon will appear on your home screen like any other app shortcut or widget, so you can drag it around and put it wherever you like:
